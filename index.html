FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How does receipt scanning work?
Take a photo of your grocery receipt. Our app reads the items and amounts, then adds them to your pantry. You can edit or remove anything before saving.
Do I need to scan every item manually?
No. Receipt scan adds many items at once. You can also scan barcodes in bulk when you get home. Manual entry is there if you need it.
Where do recipe ideas come from?
We match your inventory to a large recipe database. You see full recipes you can make with what you have, with options to filter by diet and time.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your inventory and receipts are stored securely and we don’t sell your data. You can export or delete your data anytime.
Can I use PantryPilot with my family?
Free supports one household. Pro lets you share one pantry with family members so everyone can update and see the same inventory and lists.

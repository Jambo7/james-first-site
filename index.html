About

This app lets you scan the barcodes of items you buy at the food shop, or take a photo of your receipt. It automatically updates your kitchen inventory so you always know what you have. Based on what’s in stock, it suggests full recipe ideas so you can cook with what’s on hand and cut down on waste.

Blog

Tips, updates, and ideas for smarter kitchen management. Learn how to get the most from barcode scanning and receipt uploads, and discover new ways to use your inventory for quick, easy meals.

Mission

We want to make it easy to know what’s in your kitchen and to use it well. By scanning barcodes or snapping your receipt, you keep inventory without the hassle. Recipe ideas based on your stock help you cook more and waste less—so food stays in your kitchen, not in the bin.

About PantryPilot

The story behind the app that keeps your kitchen in sync and your meals on track.

The problem

Most of us have been there: you forget what’s in the fridge, buy duplicates, or let good food go bad. Manual lists don’t keep up. Recipe apps don’t know what you actually have. The result is wasted food, wasted money, and more stress than your kitchen should bring.

Why PantryPilot

We built PantryPilot so your inventory updates with minimal effort. Scan barcodes at the shop or snap a photo of your receipt—we add the items for you. The app tracks what you have and when it expires, then suggests full recipes you can make right now. No more guessing, no more duplicate buys, and less food in the bin.

Who it’s for

PantryPilot is for anyone who wants a clearer picture of their kitchen: busy families, meal preppers, people cutting food waste, and home cooks who’d rather spend time cooking than maintaining spreadsheets. If you want to cook more with what you have and waste less, PantryPilot is for you.

Our mission

Less waste. Better cooking. One kitchen at a time.

Principles

  • Make it effortless. Inventory should update in seconds—scan or snap, then forget it.
  • Cook with what you have. Recipe ideas that match your pantry, not the other way around.
  • Reduce waste. Use-by visibility and smart suggestions so food gets eaten, not binned.
  • Respect your data. Your kitchen data is yours. We keep it private and secure.

What we’re building

We’re building the kitchen layer that was missing: a single place that knows what you have, reminds you what’s expiring, and tells you what you can cook. Receipt and barcode scanning are just the start. We’re working on smarter expiry handling, shared family pantries, and recipe suggestions that get better the more you use them—so every home can run a leaner, smarter kitchen.
